# **App Name**: My CarWash App

## Core Features:

- User Profile Management: Customers can create and manage their personal information, vehicles, and service addresses.
- Service Selection: Allow users to explore a catalog of car wash packages and add-ons to customize their order.
- Appointment Scheduling: Enable customers to select a preferred date and time for their car wash service.
- Secure Payment Processing: Integrate with Square to securely process payments within the application.
- Order History: Provide customers with access to a history of their past and current car wash orders.
- Real-time Notifications: Send updates to customers regarding the status of their car wash orders.
- Integrated Chat: Facilitate direct communication between customers and car wash personnel for seamless coordination.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Dark blue (#0A192F) for a clean and modern look.
- Secondary color: Light blue (#64FFDA) as an accent color for buttons and highlights.
- Background color: Dark grey (#112240) to provide contrast and readability.
- Body font: 'Roboto' for a clear and readable text.
- Headline font: 'Montserrat' for a modern and bold look.
- Use of simple and modern icons to represent different car wash services and features.
- A clean, card-based layout that prioritizes ease of use and scannability.
- Subtle animations and transitions to enhance user engagement and provide visual feedback on interactions.
